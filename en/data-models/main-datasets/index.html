Main datasets

D4_study_source_population

The unit of observation of this table is all the persons that have an observation period overlapping 01-01-2020.




D3_study_population

In this table, dates of vaccination (if any) are included, as well as covariates (age, at-risk conditions) at the three times: study entry date and date vax 1

* risk_factor may be equal to: cancer, diabetes, obesity, cardiovascular disease, chronic lung disease, chronic kidney disease, hiv, or sickle cell disease.




D3_vaccin_cohort

Unit of observation: persons in D3 study_population with date_vax1 non missing.

*risk_factor may be equal to: cancer, diabetes, obesity, cardiovascular disease, chronic lung disease, chronic kidney disease, hiv, or sickle cell disease.




D3_vaxweeks

Unit of observation: persons in D3 vaccin_cohort. Observations: one per week since date_vax_1 to study_exit_date (including partial weeks, if any).
